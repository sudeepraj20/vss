How We Work

Our Manufacturing Process

01

Consultation & Design

We visit your site, analyze requirements, take initial dimensions, and present custom 2D/3D design options and transparent pricing details.

02

Precision Cutting & Steel reinforcement

Using advanced machinery, the uPVC profiles are cut to exact angles, and heavy-duty galvanized steel is inserted inside the profiles for structural integrity.

03

Fusion Welding & Cleaning

Corner joints undergo high-temperature fusion welding to create a single leak-proof unit, followed by precise automated seam cleaning for clean, smooth joints.

04

Glazing & Hardware Installation

High-performance double/single glass units are glazed, and premium multi-point locks, hinges, and friction stays are fitted and tested for smooth operation.

05

On-Site Installation

Our dedicated crew handles secure transport, alignment, silicone insulation, final locks check, and site cleanup to deliver a perfect product.
